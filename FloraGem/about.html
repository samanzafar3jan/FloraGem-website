Who We Are?

For four generations, the Floras have been Decatur’s premier jewelers. Since the days when Great-Grandma Flora used her precious wedding gown to create intriguing displays, we’ve shown customers a different way of looking at jewelry. Marriages have begun and anniversaries have been celebrated with Flora Gems on this same street corner for more than 150 years. And we’re just as committed to the community as it is to us, working with organizations like American Heart Association, Mt. Zion Schools Foundation, Girl Scouts, Macon County Animal Shelter, Decatur Sympho and Junior Welfare Association to help make sure the community stays as strong as Flora Gems.



Time passes on; styles come and go, but diamonds are forever—and so is Flora Gems.
